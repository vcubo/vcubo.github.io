Integration

We process the data to make it useful

At no charge, we process projects' schedules, risk registers, and changelogs.
After a standardization and anonymization process, we integrate the data into our database. A greater universe of projects contributes to understanding better the drivers of your project’s performance.

Your projects' data will remain anonymous and safe.

First step: Wrangling projects' data. All Companies has their toolsets, templates, and project controls procedures. We process the documents and files containing the information that our models need:

  1. Baselines, updates and final schedules for each project.
  2. Risk registers.
  3. Changelogs, and more.

Second step: We anonymize project/companies' sensitive information to protect their privacy.

Third step: We integrate all the anonymized projects' data into our database.
